Hello, I am Ashley Elizabeth Taylor.

Allow me to introduce myself. My name is Ashley E. Taylor, and I will be receiving my BFA in Graphic Design and a minor in Accounting from Marshall University in May of 2016. During my last 4 years in school I have not only been able to learn about a field I enjoy, but to also go out and build my skills through various outside opportunities. I have a passion for working with the community, and like to stay involved in the design process as much as I can.


I enjoy art forms of all kinds from photography, to painting, to pint design, to digital mediums. Art is and will always be my life, whether I am working the creative side, the business side, or some combination of the two. I am proud of any job I do, and no project is too insignificant.


Please take some time to look through my portfolio, or flip over to my flickr to see more. Feel free to contact me anytime with any questions you might have.
